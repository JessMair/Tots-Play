About Us

Tots Play is a family run soft play faclity for babies and toddlers.
The facility offers many opportunities for play and interactions, even for the smallest of our visitors. From sensory play to our newly refurbished soft play jungle gym, to much more in between. We also offer sensory sessions for babies. A gentle way to introduce your baby to thier senses and what they do.
Tots Play is great place to meet other parents and socialise whist the little ones run wild. You can also enjoy a coffee or one of the many delcious dishes from our on site cafe.
